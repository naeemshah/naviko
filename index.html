Immigration lawyers are valuable for complex legal strategy. But most of what they charge for on simple applications is document review — and that's exactly what AI is built for.

Rules that are always current

Immigration rules change without announcement. USCIS updates processing requirements mid-week. IRCC adjusts Express Entry CRS cutoffs with two days' notice. Naviko monitors official government sources continuously — so your checklist reflects today's rules, not last year's blog post.

AI that reads your documents the way an officer does

Our AI doesn't just check that a file uploaded successfully. It reads the content — names, dates, formats, authorities, specifications — and cross-references it against the exact criteria for your visa type. The same things a government officer checks. Caught before submission, not after.

A green light you can actually trust

Naviko's readiness indicator is not a progress bar. It is a document-by-document compliance check. Every item on your checklist has a status. When every status is green, your application is ready. When something is red, you know exactly what it is and exactly how to fix it.

Guidance you understand — not legal jargon

Every requirement is explained in plain language. Every document has a detailed guide: what it is, why it's needed, what it must contain, and what the most common rejection reasons are. You leave knowing your application — not just trusting that someone else checked it.
